# 📊 Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance using Excel and Power BI to identify sales trends, profitability drivers, discount impacts, and regional sales performance. The goal was to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, KPI analysis, and interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals
- Analyze sales and profit trends
- Identify high and low-performing categories
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability
- Explore sales distribution across countries
- Build an interactive and professional business dashboard

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders

### Interactive Visualizations
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Profit by Category
- Profit vs Discount
- Sales by Country
- Top Sub-Categories

### Filters & Interactivity
- Year Slicer
- Top Countries Filter

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales peaked during Q4.
- Technology generated the highest profit.
- High discounts negatively impacted profitability.
- Some sub-categories showed low or negative profit margins.
- Sales performance varied significantly across countries.

---

## 📌 Recommendations
- Reduce excessive discounts on low-margin products.
- Focus on high-performing categories such as Technology.
- Improve pricing strategies for underperforming products.
- Monitor profitability by region more closely.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Power BI – Interactive dashboard creation
- DAX – KPI and measure calculations
